About Us

OUR STORY

Ardee's isn't just a restaurant; it's a story of passion, commitment, and a love for great food. Founded by a group of friends in 2023, Ardee's started as a humble food truck parked at local events and festivals. With a shared vision of serving mouthwatering meals made with the freshest ingredients, Ardee's quickly gained popularity among food enthusiasts.
